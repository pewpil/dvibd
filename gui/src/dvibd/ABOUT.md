Dvibd (*dvibd* stylized) is a social networking company that provides products and services that connects other people. Such product/services are *social*, *dvibd*'s social media and *message*, *dvibd*'s communication chat app. *dvibd* is also a company that serves productivity tools such as note taking, cloud, email, document editing, etc. This directory is the frontend source code of its company site wrapped around by universal entry point, `@src/index.tsx` .
